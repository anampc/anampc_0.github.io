I am a biologist interested in the future of our oceans. My main focus of research has been how ocean warming, acidification, and pollution affect corals and disrupt the coral-algal symbiosis, causing coral bleaching and mortality. I am also trying to identify factors that promote more resistant corals that could survive in our fast changing world.

My research is focused on improving coral restoration efforts by providing information about the advantages and risks of trait selection.
  • I investigate the genetic basis of disease resistance in the threatened Caribbean staghorn coral, Acropora cervicornis.
  • I also assess potential trade-offs associated with disease resistance and how the environment shapes these tradeoffs.
